Lasagna

How to make lasagna in 45 minutes!

Lasagna is one of the most liked recipes in the world. We have made a simple recipe, so you can go impress your friends, without having to stand in the kitchen all day

Even though the recipe is made simple, it is very important, that you stick to the recipe from start to finish

Ingredients

  • 500gr meat
  • 600gr Pasta
  • 2 cans of tomatosauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Add tomatosauce to the meat
  3. Put the mix of meat and tomatosauce in a platter
