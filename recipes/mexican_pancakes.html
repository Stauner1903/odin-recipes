Mexican Pancakes

The best way to make your mexican pancakes stand out!

Mexican pancakes are simple, and they can get boring too. This guide will make sure, that every person you serve it to, will be beggin for the recipe.

Even though the recipe is simple, it's still very important, that you stay focused and stick to the recipe from start to finish

Ingredients

  • 500gr Chicken
  • 4 Pancakes
  • 2 ts Chillisauce

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Add Chillisauce to the meat
  3. Serve the chicken with pancakes and vegatables
